# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

**Between**

**Mission Distinction** (hereinafter referred to as "the Platform")

**And**

**[Name of Medical College / Institution]**, [City], Odisha
(hereinafter referred to as "the Institution")

---

*This Memorandum of Understanding ("MoU") is entered into on this ______ day of _____________, 202_.*

---

## PREAMBLE

**WHEREAS**, Mission Distinction is a free, technology-driven medical education platform specifically designed for 1st Year MBBS students, offering gamified learning, AI-powered academic tools, community features, digital study resources, and performance analytics;

**WHEREAS**, the Institution is a recognised medical college in the State of Odisha, affiliated with [Shri Ram Chandra Bhanj Medical University / Sambalpur University / other], committed to providing quality medical education to its students;

**WHEREAS**, both parties recognize that innovative digital tools can significantly enhance student learning outcomes, academic preparedness, and engagement;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual benefits and obligations described herein, both parties agree to the following terms:

---

## ARTICLE 1 — DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Platform"** means the Mission Distinction web application, Progressive Web App (PWA), and all associated services accessible at [app URL].

1.2 **"Students"** means the enrolled MBBS students (1st Year) of the Institution.

1.3 **"Content"** means study materials, PDFs, books, quizzes, flashcards, mnemonics, and any other academic content available on the Platform.

1.4 **"Partnership Period"** means the duration of this MoU as defined in Article 7.

1.5 **"Institutional Administrator"** means the designated faculty member(s) appointed by the Institution to manage and monitor Platform activities.

---

## ARTICLE 2 — PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

2.1 The purpose of this MoU is to establish a formal academic partnership between Mission Distinction and the Institution to:

  (a) Provide all enrolled 1st Year MBBS students of the Institution with **free, unrestricted access** to the Mission Distinction platform;

  (b) Supplement classroom teaching with digital study tools including AI-assisted doubt resolution, curated mnemonics, practice quizzes, and subject-specific study resources for Anatomy, Physiology, and Biochemistry;

  (c) Foster a culture of **engaged, self-paced, and collaborative learning** through gamification, leaderboards, and peer study rooms;

  (d) Enable faculty to monitor student academic engagement and progress through the Platform's administrative dashboard;

  (e) Jointly work toward improved academic outcomes and better preparation for University examinations.

---

## ARTICLE 3 — SCOPE OF COLLABORATION

### 3.1 Digital Learning Access
The Platform shall provide all students of the Institution with full, free access to:
- Subject-specific PDFs and digital books (Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry)
- AI-powered doubt-solving assistant (24/7 availability)
- Practice quizzes with instant feedback and performance analytics
- Flashcard sets and official AI-generated mnemonics
- Gamified XP and rank system to motivate consistent study
- Community study rooms and peer discussion forums
- Study music hub and focus tools
- Personal notes and exam calendar

### 3.2 Institutional Administration Panel
- A dedicated administrator account shall be provided to the Institution's designated faculty representative
- The admin panel shall allow content management, student progress monitoring, quiz creation, and announcement publishing

### 3.3 Institutional Branding
- The Institution's name and logo shall be displayed within the Platform as a **Partnered Institution**
- A dedicated landing page or welcome message for the Institution's students shall be created on the Platform

### 3.4 Content Collaboration (Optional)
- Faculty members of the Institution may optionally contribute curated academic content, case studies, or quiz questions to the Platform
- Such contributed content shall be credited to the contributing faculty/institution

### 3.5 Events and Competitions
- The Platform shall host institution-specific leaderboards, quiz competitions, and academic events in coordination with the Institution
- The Institution may request customised quiz sets aligned with its internal examination schedule

---

## ARTICLE 4 — OBLIGATIONS OF MISSION DISTINCTION

Mission Distinction agrees to:

4.1 Provide **100% free access** to the Platform for all enrolled students of the Institution for the entire duration of this MoU — no subscription fees, no hidden charges, no advertisements;

4.2 Maintain **platform uptime of at least 95%** and ensure technical support is available via email within 48 working hours;

4.3 Keep all student data **confidential and secure**, stored on India-based servers, and never sell or share personal data with third parties;

4.4 Provide the Institution's designated Institutional Administrator with a **monthly engagement report** summarising student activity, quiz performance, and platform usage statistics;

4.5 Ensure the Platform complies with applicable Indian data protection laws including the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023**;

4.6 Notify the Institution of any significant platform changes, downtime, or content updates at least 5 working days in advance;

4.7 Display the Institution's name as an **Official Partner** on the Platform and in any promotional material (with prior written consent of the Institution);

4.8 Onboard students of the Institution through a **streamlined registration process** (bulk invite or institutional email domain verification) within 14 working days of signing this MoU.

---

## ARTICLE 5 — OBLIGATIONS OF THE INSTITUTION

The Institution agrees to:

5.1 Officially **endorse Mission Distinction** as a recommended digital learning resource for 1st Year MBBS students of the Institution;

5.2 Appoint a **designated faculty member** as Institutional Administrator to coordinate with the Platform team and monitor student engagement;

5.3 **Facilitate student onboarding** by communicating the Platform's availability to students through official channels (notice board, student orientation, class representatives);

5.4 Permit Mission Distinction to display the Institution's name and logo in the Platform and associated promotional materials, subject to written approval of the design/usage;

5.5 Provide reasonable cooperation in sharing anonymised, aggregated **academic calendar information** (examination dates, syllabus schedule) to allow the Platform to tailor content delivery accordingly;

5.6 Participate in at least **one joint academic event** (e.g., inter-college quiz competition, digital learning orientation) per academic year organised through the Platform;

5.7 Provide honest feedback to Mission Distinction on platform utility, content quality, and areas for improvement on a **half-yearly basis**.

---

## ARTICLE 6 — FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENTS

6.1 This MoU is entered into on a **strictly non-commercial, pro-bono basis**. No fees, charges, or financial obligations are imposed on the Institution or its students by Mission Distinction.

6.2 Each party shall bear its own expenses incurred in furtherance of activities under this MoU.

6.3 Should any joint event or activity require shared funding, the financial arrangement shall be agreed upon separately in writing by both parties prior to the event.

---

## ARTICLE 7 — DURATION AND RENEWAL

7.1 This MoU shall come into force on the date of signing and shall remain valid for a period of **Two (2) Academic Years** from the date of execution.

7.2 Upon expiry, this MoU may be renewed by mutual written consent of both parties for a further period as agreed.

7.3 Either party may review and renegotiate the terms of this MoU at the end of each academic year, with at least 30 days' written notice to the other party.

---

## ARTICLE 8 — CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1 Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information, student data, or institutional data shared in the course of this partnership.

8.2 Neither party shall disclose the other party's confidential information to any third party without prior written consent, except as required by law.

8.3 This obligation of confidentiality shall survive the termination or expiry of this MoU for a period of **Two (2) years**.

---

## ARTICLE 9 — DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

9.1 Mission Distinction shall collect only such personal data from students as is necessary for the operation of the Platform (name, email, phone number, academic progress).

9.2 All personal data shall be stored on **India-based servers** and handled in accordance with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** and applicable University Grants Commission (UGC) guidelines.

9.3 Students shall be informed of the data collected and their rights thereto through a clear Privacy Policy at the time of registration.

9.4 Mission Distinction shall not share identifiable student data with any third party, advertiser, or government body except as required by law.

9.5 Upon termination of this MoU, Mission Distinction shall provide the Institution with an export of all student data upon request and permanently delete the same within 90 days, unless students individually opt to retain their accounts.

---

## ARTICLE 10 — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

10.1 All intellectual property including software, design, gamification systems, content, and brand elements developed by Mission Distinction shall remain the exclusive property of Mission Distinction.

10.2 All academic content, case studies, or quiz questions contributed by the Institution's faculty shall remain the intellectual property of the contributing faculty member / Institution, with a non-exclusive licence granted to Mission Distinction to display such content on the Platform during the term of this MoU.

10.3 Neither party shall use the other's trademarks, logos, or brand names beyond what is expressly permitted under this MoU without prior written consent.

---

## ARTICLE 11 — TERMINATION

11.1 Either party may terminate this MoU by providing **30 days' written notice** to the other party, without assigning any reason.

11.2 Either party may immediately terminate this MoU in the event of:
  (a) A material breach of this MoU by the other party that remains uncured for 15 days after written notice of such breach;
  (b) Any conduct that brings disrepute to either party;
  (c) Insolvency, dissolution, or cessation of operations of either party.

11.3 Upon termination, both parties shall immediately cease using the other's intellectual property and return or destroy any confidential information as applicable.

---

## ARTICLE 12 — DISPUTE RESOLUTION

12.1 Both parties shall endeavour to resolve any disputes arising out of or in connection with this MoU through **mutual discussion and good faith negotiation** within 30 days of the dispute arising.

12.2 If the dispute is not resolved through negotiation, it shall be referred to a mutually appointed sole **Mediator**.

12.3 If mediation fails, the dispute shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of **Bhubaneswar, Odisha**.

---

## ARTICLE 13 — GENERAL PROVISIONS

13.1 **Entire Agreement**: This MoU constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions and understandings.

13.2 **Amendment**: This MoU may be amended only by a written instrument signed by authorised representatives of both parties.

13.3 **Severability**: If any provision of this MoU is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.

13.4 **Waiver**: Failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver of that provision.

13.5 **Governing Law**: This MoU shall be governed by the laws of India.

13.6 **Non-Exclusivity**: This MoU is non-exclusive. Mission Distinction may partner with other institutions, and the Institution may use other digital learning platforms simultaneously.

---
