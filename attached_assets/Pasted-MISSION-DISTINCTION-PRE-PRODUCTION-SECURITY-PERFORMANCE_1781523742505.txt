MISSION DISTINCTION — PRE-PRODUCTION SECURITY, PERFORMANCE & BUG AUDIT

Perform a complete production-readiness audit of the entire Mission Distinction codebase.

Your task is to act as a senior software architect, penetration tester, QA engineer, DevOps engineer, and security auditor.

DO NOT add new features unless required to fix bugs or vulnerabilities.
